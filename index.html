Frequently Asked Questions

Do you only work with US clients?
Yes. We are fully US-based and specialize exclusively in US-compliant strategies.
What is the minimum portfolio size?
We typically work with clients who have $750,000+ in digital assets.
How are fees structured?
Management fees are performance-based with a low fixed component. Full transparency guaranteed.
Are your strategies compliant with current US regulations?
Yes. All recommendations strictly adhere to SEC, CFTC, FinCEN, and IRS guidelines. We track evolving regulations (including the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, proposed SAB 121 changes, and upcoming 1099-DA broker reporting rules) and adjust client strategies accordingly.
Do you provide custody or hold client assets?
No. We are a pure advisory firm. We do not take custody of funds or assets. We work with institutional-grade qualified custodians (such as Coinbase Custody, Fidelity Digital Assets, Anchorage Digital, or Fireblocks) that our clients already use or that we help select.
What kind of performance data do you share?
We provide personalized performance attribution and risk-adjusted return analysis for each client. Publicly, we share only anonymized, aggregate, time-weighted returns that comply with GIPS-like standards and are verified by third-party accountants when disclosed.
How do you handle crypto tax reporting for US clients?
We provide detailed transaction-level cost-basis tracking, FIFO/LIFO/HIFO optimization recommendations, wash-sale identification (where applicable), and prepare client-ready data exports compatible with TurboTax, CoinTracker, Koinly, and professional tax software. We do not file taxes directly but work closely with your CPA.
What happens if I want to pause or terminate the advisory relationship?
You may terminate at any time with 30 days’ written notice. There are no lock-up periods or early termination fees on monthly plans. Upon termination we provide a final portfolio report and 90 days of continued access to our client portal for transition purposes.
Do you offer personalized on-chain security audits?
Yes — for Professional and Enterprise clients. We perform wallet lineage analysis, address clustering checks, exposure scanning (OFAC/SDN lists, mixer/tumbler history), and recommend multi-sig / MPC setups when appropriate.
How confidential is my information?
Extremely. We operate under strict NDAs, use end-to-end encryption, zero-knowledge proofs for certain reporting workflows, and maintain SOC 2 Type II controls. We never sell data and we are not subject to routine subpoena fishing expeditions common with custodial platforms.
