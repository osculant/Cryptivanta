Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority.

Cryptivanta LLC ("Cryptivanta", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our elite crypto consulting services for high-net-worth and institutional clients in the United States.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, financial details (portfolio size, investment goals, risk tolerance), tax identification, and any information provided during consultations or onboarding.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and site usage collected via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized crypto portfolio management, DeFi strategy, risk analysis, and US tax & compliance guidance.
  • Communicate with you about services, reports, and market insights.
  • Comply with legal obligations as a US-based crypto advisory firm (including AML, KYC, and regulatory reporting).

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (qualified custodians, tax software platforms, analytics providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law (SEC, IRS, FinCEN, etc.).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 controls, and zero-knowledge technologies where applicable. However, no system is 100% secure, especially in the evolving crypto landscape.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. California residents may have additional rights under CCPA/CPRA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for website functionality. You can manage preferences through your browser settings.
